## ğŸ® **Virtual Game Mechanics**

### **Option A: Breakout Room Style (Recommended)**

#### **Setup:**
- 6 breakout rooms with 5 people each
- Each room gets their own digital whiteboard
- Facilitator "visits" rooms to give words and check progress
- Teams compete against each other

#### **How It Works:**
1. **Round Start:** Facilitator broadcasts word to all rooms via chat
2. **Drawing Phase:** One person per team draws on their whiteboard (90 seconds)
3. **Guessing Phase:** Team members guess in their breakout room
4. **Scoring:** Teams report back to main room with their score

### **Option B: Main Room Spotlight Style**

#### **Setup:**
- Everyone stays in main room
- Teams take turns being "on stage"
- One shared whiteboard visible to all
- More spectator-friendly but less participation

#### **How It Works:**
1. Call up "Team 1" 
2. One person shares screen and draws
3. Their team guesses (mics on)
4. Everyone else watches and cheers
5. Rotate through all 6 teams
